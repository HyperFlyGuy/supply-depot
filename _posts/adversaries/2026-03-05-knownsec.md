# Knownsec Analysis

## Summary:

This analysis is largely based on a leak of Knownsec that was published in November of 2025. While most atomic indicators would have long faded from relevancy, we are hoping there is additional information in the leak that could lead to new hunt ideas or TTPs that are associated with the group. With the tooling described below they essentially have a full intrusion/surveillance pipeline that support long-term access, DNS hijacking, admin takeover, and infrastructure control.

Knownsec has been marketed as a white hat pillar of the Chinese government, but internal processes and documentation reveal a complex relationship that shows them as offensive operators for the Chinese government. It operates within a peer group of organizations (see below), however, Knownsec stands out due to the integration of its products and their ability to produce an entire operational pipeline for offensive cyber operations.

## Observations:

- Large organization with multiple departments that align with PRC goals and objectives
- Extensive recon and correlation capabilities that allow targeted operations against organizations. 



## Capabilities:

Knownsec is an extremely structured and coordinated group that is able thoroughly recon and correlate their targets, gain initial access, and maintain persistence. 

Passive Radar - T1040: Network Sniffing
Un-mail = T1041: Exfiltration over C2, T1071: Application Layer Protocol
Ghostx - T056: Input Capture, T1059: Command Execution, T1185: Browser Exploitation


## Tooling:

- **Ghostx** - Browser exploitation, routing manipulation, credential theft, endpoint monitoring
- **Un-Mail** - take over and continuous exfiltration of email accounts across global providers
- **Passive Radar** - Ingests PCAP data via local uploads, FTP, and SSH, to reconstruct internal network topology, communication patterns, and service inventories
- **ZoomEye** - A global cyberspace search engine equivalent to Shodan/FOFA but with:
  - Full IPv4-space scanning
  - 40,000+ component fingerprints
  - Rapid recrawl cycles (7–10 days)
  - Cross-integration with TargetDB
- **TargetDB** - an immense, curated intelligence repository that transforms raw internet data into a structured map of global critical infrastructure.
- **Data Lake (o_data_)** - A carefully indexed archive of global breach data, sourced from various breach resources. It acts as a correlation engine for the data gathered from Zoomeye/TargetDB to enrich targets even further.
